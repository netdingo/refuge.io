The refuge box

The refuge box will act as a gateway for your data coming from devices, people or services. It will allow you to easily collect and store them and connect to other box and people around over the refuge box

The refuge box will be available in 2014

But first, a refuge box prototype

While the refuge box still in development, we need your help. As always, we believe the best way to ensure that the right products are getting made is to let people try them out and then make changes as we go. We have designed a prototype that’s optimized for the refuge data platform. it’s also completely upgradable and open.

In november 2013, we will release our first design. Keep posted!
