refuge, the data social platform

Refuge connects data sources between machines and people, coming from devices, peoples or services in a decentralized manner.

A number of individuals are collecting large amounts of data provided by environmental sensors or other sources. However, this data is too often underutilized by the greater community. The goal of this project is to build an open source tool that will allow to collect data and share them easily in a distributed and decentralized manner.

Data is yours

Your data is your own, share and store it on your terms. With refuge you can choose to store your data on the cloud or on your own infrastructure or disk. Then choose to share all, some or none of your it with others or the world at large.

Decentralized

Easily coordinate multiple data sources coming from devices, peoples or services through the P2P refuge platform using the refuge hubs provided by the community or by connecting directly to refuge nodes.

Connect

Build a true Internet of things by connecting your sensors, devices or any others data source to the refuge platform.
