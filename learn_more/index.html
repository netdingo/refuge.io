refuge, the data social platform

Refuge connects data sources between machines and people, coming from devices, peoples or services in a decentralized manner.

A number of individuals are collecting large amounts of data provided by environmental sensors or other sources. However, this data is too often underutilized by the greater community. The goal of this project is to build an open source tool that will allows to collect data and share them easily a distributed and decentralized manner.
